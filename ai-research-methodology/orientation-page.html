Essential Setup: Claude Project Configuration

‚è±Ô∏è Time required: 30-45 minutes

This is the only setup required to begin using AI collaboration for your dissertation work. Everything else is optional enhancement.

Step 1: Subscribe to Claude Pro (5 minutes)

  1. Navigate to claude.ai
  2. Create account or log in
  3. Subscribe to Claude Pro ($20/month)
  4. Verify Projects feature is available in left sidebar

‚úì You're ready when you see "Projects" in the sidebar

Step 2: Create Your Dissertation Project (5 minutes)

  1. Click "Projects" in left sidebar
  2. Click "+ New Project" button
  3. Name it: "Dissertation - [Your Topic]"
    Example: "Dissertation - Supervisory Relationships"
  4. Click "Create Project"

‚úì You now have a dedicated workspace for your research
